# Live Music World® – APK Installation for Android 

## How to install the app on your Android phone or tablet

1. **Download the APK file**  
   - Tap the download link you received (email, message, or GitHub).  
   - The file will save to your **Downloads** folder.

2. **Tap the APK file**  
   - Open your **Downloads** folder or tap the notification that says "Download complete".  
   - Tap the file named `LiveMusicWorld.apk` (or similar).

3. **Allow installation if asked**  
   - A pop‑up may say: *"Your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps"* – tap **"Settings"**.  
   - Find your browser or file manager and toggle **"Allow from this source"** to ON.  
   - Then go back and tap the APK again.  
   - (On most modern Androids, just tap **"Install"** – no extra steps.)

4. **Tap "Install"**  
   - Wait 2–3 seconds.  
   - Tap **"Open"** to launch Live Music World®.

5. **Done** – the app icon appears on your home screen.

## Notes
- Internet is required for streaming radio.  
- If the app doesn't open, go to **Settings → Apps** and find Live Music World, then tap **"Open"**.  
- To uninstall: press and hold the icon → **Uninstall**.
